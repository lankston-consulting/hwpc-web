06Enter number of Monte Carlo iterations

Monte Carlo Disclaimer:

IPCC provides information on potential sources of uncertainty associated with the estimates of CO2 emissions and removals arising from HWP. HWPC model's algorithm is deterministic, but requires recursion to simulate recycled materials re-entering use. This makes HWPC computationally expensive and time-consuming. If activated, a Monte Carlo simulation of HWPC will exponentially increase the number of iterations that must be run and will take an extremely long time to run.

Unless your reporting requires measurement of uncertainty, Monte Carlo simulations of HWPC are not necessary.
